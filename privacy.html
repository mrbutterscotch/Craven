Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Butterscotch Studios handles information for Craven and butterscotchstudios.org.

Summary

Craven only saves the information needed to create, authenticate, and maintain a game account. We do not sell personal information, use advertising trackers, run analytics services, or work with third-party services that collect player data.

Information We Collect

Craven may store basic account information required for sign-in, such as an account identifier, username or display name, and authentication data. We do not collect personal information beyond what is needed to create and operate the account.

How We Use Information

We use account information only to let players create an account, sign in, access the game, and keep the game service working securely.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect contacts, location, photos, payment information, advertising identifiers, or data from other apps. This website does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics.

Third-Party Services

Craven does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, payment, or player-data services.

Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Retention

Account information is kept only as long as needed to maintain the account and operate Craven. When an account is deleted, account data is removed from active systems unless retention is required for security, abuse prevention, or legal reasons.

Children's Privacy

Craven is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes

If our privacy practices change, we will update this policy and revise the date above.
